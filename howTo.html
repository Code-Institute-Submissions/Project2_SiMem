Hey welcome to SiMem!
The below is a quick run through of how to play the game.
  • To play, click on the Power button. It's located next to "Power" adjacent the level display
  • Press Start to initiate the game
  • Click on the squares that light up after SiMem has finished to proceed to the next level
  • If you get anything wrong, wait a second for SiMem to replay the sequence again
  • Get to 6 and you win!
  • Good Luck!
